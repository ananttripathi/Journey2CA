# Journey2CA

A structured platform for CA aspirants — Foundation, Intermediate & Final (New 2023 Scheme).b

## What is Journey2CA?

Journey2CA helps CA students navigate their exam preparation with structured study plans, chapter-wise notes, practice questions, and progress tracking — all in one place.

**Core differentiator:** Smart study plans that adapt to your exam date and track your progress chapter by chapter.

## Who is this for?

Students preparing for ICAI CA exams under the **New 2023 Scheme** across all three levels:
- CA Foundation
- CA Intermediate
- CA Final

## Key Features (Planned)

- **Study Plan Generator** — Input your exam date, get a day-wise chapter schedule
- **Chapter-wise Notes** — Theory, formulas, solved examples, past exam Qs
- **Inline Practice** — Quick MCQs with instant feedback after every chapter
- **Progress Tracker** — Mark chapters complete, visualise subject-wise completion
- **Amendment Tracker** — Finance Act & ICAI updates flagged on relevant chapters
- **Mock Tests** — Timed subject-wise and full mock papers
- **Doubt Forum** — Community-driven Q&A

## Content Structure

All content is organised by level → paper → chapter.

| Level | Papers | Syllabus |
|---|---|---|
| [Foundation](content/foundation.md) | 4 papers | New 2023 Scheme |
| [Intermediate](content/intermediate.md) | 8 papers (2 groups) | New 2023 Scheme |
| [Final](content/final.md) | 6 papers (2 groups) | New 2023 Scheme |

## Platform

- Web-first (mobile app coming later)
- Public content — no login required to read notes and chapters
- Login required for study plans, progress tracking, and mock test scores
